Pantera

Biografía

Pantera was an American heavy metal band formed in 1981 in Arlington, Texas. The group's best-known lineup consisted of the Abbott brothers (drummer Vinnie Paul and guitarist Dimebag Darrell), along with vocalist Phil Anselmo and bassist Rex Brown. Pantera is considered one of the most successful and influential bands in heavy metal history, known for their aggressive sound and technical proficiency.
